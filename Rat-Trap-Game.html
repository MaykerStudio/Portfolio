"Rat Trap" is a 2D Metroidvania game that blends intricate puzzle-solving, character combination mechanics, and fast-paced combat. As the main programmer, I led the development of the game's core systems, including complex character interactions, combat mechanics, and puzzle dynamics, ensuring smooth and engaging gameplay. I worked closely with the design and art teams to integrate seamless level progression and responsive controls, delivering a cohesive and challenging player experience. This project showcases my expertise in building full games, systems, mechanics, menus, and technical VFX.
